Startup Procedure

Run these steps in order at the start of a service: power the racks, connect the desk computers, set the panel, recall the routing, then verify. Each step assumes the one before it is done.

1

Power on the racks & the ATEM panel

Brings up venue devices, rack gear, and the switcher panel
  1. Power on the Furman in the rack — this powers up all of the venue devices.
  2. Power on the Middle Atlantic rack-mount power conditioner.
  3. Boot up the Resi decoder and the console interface in the rack.
  4. Physically reconnect power to the ATEM Advanced Panel. The venue disconnects its power between services, and the panel has no power switch — it boots as soon as the power cable is connected. Do this early so it's fully up by Step 3.
2

Connect the desk computers to the AJA ROI converters

Stacked AJA ROI converters underneath the desk · match by SDI cable colour

Connect an HDMI cable from each Elevation Macbook to its AJA ROI converter in the stack under the desk. Until the boxes are labelled, use the coloured SDI cables as the reference for which converter belongs to which computer:

SDI cableComputerLands on
WhiteResolume Mac (EC2)Videohub IN 35
YellowCG2 Mac (EC3)Videohub IN 36
OrangeCG1 Mac (EC4)Videohub IN 37
Box labels are pending — the colour match is the interim reference. Verify the result in Step 5.
3

Restore ATEM Profile 44

ATEM 2 M/E Advanced Panel 20 · reloads Elevation panel mapping, preferences & macros
  1. Press the soft PROFILE button above the LCD, then the right arrow in system control to reach the profiles page.
  2. Turn the soft control knob to Profile 44.
  3. Press the soft RESTORE button above the LCD.
Orange text above the slot confirms Profile 44 is now in use. The published manual lists a 10-profile limit, but the panel firmware holds more and 44 was saved on the physical panel — trust it's there. If the slot ever reads empty, it needs re-saving.
4

Recall Videohub Routing Macros

Videohub 40x40 12G front panel · each macro button lights green, then TAKE applies it
Standard startup — Elevation macros (×2):
STREAM→TAKE then CAM→TAKE

Press each macro button (illuminates green), then press TAKE to confirm that macro's crosspoints before moving to the next. If a macro can't be performed the button flashes.

After a dicey rental — run River first. If the venue was left in an unknown state, restore River's general functionality before honing in on ours. Fire the River macros (×3), then run the Elevation macros above on top:
MON→TAKE · DECK→TAKE · EDIT→TAKE
5

Verify inputs on the Videohub display

Videohub 40x40 12G front-panel video preview · do this after firing the macros

Use the video display on the Videohub front panel to confirm each Elevation Macbook landed on the right input — check that the picture on IN 35 / 36 / 37 matches the expected computer (white → Resolume, yellow → CG2, orange → CG1). If two are swapped, re-seat the HDMI at the AJA ROI stack per the colour reference in Step 2, then re-check.

Sunday Run of Show — Expected Signal State

What each bus should be showing at each point in a normal service. Useful for telling "that's wrong" apart from "that's just where we are right now."

⚠ Terminology: in show calling, "Resolume" always means ME4 (the full-screen DVE-expanded version) — never the raw EC2 source. "Uplink" is the broadcast join with the North Carolina main campus, carried on Resi, cut in full-screen with no DVE step needed. "CG1 full screen" means CG1 cut directly as the ME3 source, not keyed.
#SegmentME1 · MakitoME2 · LobbyME3 · Main ScreenNotes
1 Pre-Experience Slides Cam 1 CG1 (slides) CG1 (slides) Baseline resting state before the experience starts.
2 Experience Start / Countdown Cam 1 Cam 1 ME4 "Resolume" (countdown) ME3 transitions CG1 → ME4. ME2 transitions CG1 → Cam 1 for the lobby feed.
3 Countdown → Worship Visuals Cam 1 Cam 1 ME4 "Resolume" + Key 1 (centre lyrics) Resolume transitions countdown → visuals internally — no ATEM cut needed. Key on ME3 Key 1 for local lyrics.
4 Welcome Cam 1 Cam 1 CG1 full screen (hosting slides) Key off Key 1, then background-transition ME4 → CG1 on ME3.
5 Back to Local Worship Cam 1 Cam 1 ME4 "Resolume" + Key 1 (centre lyrics) Transition CG1 → ME4, key on Key 1 again.
6 Uplink — song through sermon Cam 1 Uplink (Resi) full screen Resi full screen + Key 2 (top-right lyrics) ME3 transitions ME4 → Resi (no DVE step for Resi); Key 1 → Key 2. ME2 switches to Uplink full screen on the lobby top bus at some point in this window — stays this way through the sermon.
7 Coming Out of Sermon Cam 1 Not specified ME4 "Resolume" or CG1 Director's call on ME3 depending on the look/visual needed for that moment.
8 Hosting Support / Closing Cam 1 Not specified CG1 full screen Eventually lands on CG1 for hosting support through the closing elements.
1 Pre-Experience Slides (loop) Cam 1 CG1 (slides) CG1 (slides) Cycle repeats for the next service.

ME1 stays on Camera 1 for the entire service — never changes for Makito capture. ME2's state between the end of Uplink and the next Pre-Experience Slides wasn't specified; confirm with the director.

Diagnose a Symptom

Open the symptom that matches what you're seeing. Each one lists the exact hop sequence and where to look first — worked top to bottom in order.

Main Screen — black, frozen, or wrong source Lane A
ME3 PGM→ Videohub IN 23→ OUT 22→ LED Controller→ Main Screen
  1. Confirm ME3 is actually delegated to the panel's bottom bus and the intended source is taken on PGM.
  2. Check the ATEM multiview for ME3 PGM — if it's correct there, the fault is downstream of the switcher.
  3. Verify the Videohub crosspoint OUT 22 is still patched from IN 23 (not accidentally re-routed).
  4. Check LED Controller input status/signal lock, then cabling from Videohub to controller.
If ME3 PGM itself is wrong on the multiview, the problem is upstream — check whichever source is cut in (see Input Cross-Points).
Lyrics missing, wrong, or mispositioned on Main Screen Lane A
CG1 Mac / ProPresenter→ AJA ROI (orange)→ ATEM IN 9→ ME3 Key 1 (centre) / Key 2 (top, Link)→ ME3 PGM
  1. Confirm ProPresenter is actually outputting on the CG1 Macbook.
  2. Check the orange-tagged AJA ROI link status for that run.
  3. Confirm ATEM IN 9 shows signal on the multiview.
  4. On ME3: centre lyrics use Key 1, top/"Link" lyrics use Key 2 — confirm the correct key is enabled and its luma clip/gain and mask haven't drifted.
  5. Confirm key layer priority still sits above fill in the ME3 keyer stack.
Ground Panels — wrong image, frozen, or dark Lane B
Resolume Mac / EC2→ AJA ROI (white)→ Videohub IN 35→ OUT 23→ Ground Panels
  1. The ATEM is not in this path — don't spend time on ME3/ME4, this feed is a direct crosspoint.
  2. Confirm Resolume is actually outputting on the Resolume Macbook and it's the raw/untreated composition (not a full-screen DVE version — that's ME4's job, not this feed's).
  3. Check the white-tagged AJA ROI link status.
  4. Confirm Videohub crosspoint OUT 23 is still patched from IN 35.
  5. Check the LED Controller's input for the ground panels and cabling from Videohub.
Resolume graphic looks small or wrong size when used elsewhere Lane A
Raw Resolume (ATEM IN 12)→ ME4 DVE Key 1→ cut in as "ME4" on ME1/2/3
  1. This symptom means the operator likely cut to the raw EC2/Resolume source directly instead of ME4.
  2. ME4's only job is to take that raw feed and expand it full-screen via its DVE Key 1 — confirm Key 1 is enabled on ME4.
  3. On whichever M/E needs the full-screen graphic, confirm the source selected is ME4, labelled "Resolume" — not the raw input.
Lobby TVs — wrong image or dark Lane A
ME2 PGM→ Videohub IN 22→ OUT 31→ Lobby TVs
  1. Confirm ME2 is delegated to the panel's top bus and has the intended source on PGM.
  2. Check ME2 PGM on the multiview.
  3. Confirm Videohub crosspoint OUT 31 is still patched from IN 22.
  4. Check the lobby TV distribution path and individual sets.
Recording / Makito capture blank or wrong Lane A
ME1 PGM→ Videohub IN 21→ OUT 19→ Makito Encoder
  1. Confirm ME1 PGM on the multiview shows the intended capture source.
  2. Confirm Videohub crosspoint OUT 19 is still patched from IN 21.
  3. Check Makito encoder status/link independently of the video path.
Top Confidence Monitor wrong Lane B
CG2 Mac / EC3→ AJA ROI (yellow)→ Videohub IN 36→ OUT 24→ Top Confidence Monitor
  1. This bypasses the ATEM entirely — check CG2's own output first.
  2. Check the yellow-tagged AJA ROI link status.
  3. Confirm Videohub crosspoint OUT 24 is still patched from IN 36.
Bottom Confidence Monitor wrong / clock not showing Sunday Lane B
Clock Mac + BMD Mini Converter→ Videohub IN 13 ("Resolume 1")→ OUT 25→ Bottom Confidence Monitor
  1. This port is dual-purpose: it's labelled "Resolume 1" but is hijacked by the Clock Macbook on Sundays. On other days this monitor reflects the actual River Resolume 1 SDI at FOH, not a clock.
  2. If the clock isn't showing on a Sunday, confirm the BMD Mini Converter is connected and the Clock Macbook is set to that output.
  3. Confirm Videohub crosspoint OUT 25 is still patched from IN 13.
No console audio from Resi or CG1 Lane B
Resi Decoder (EC1) → IN 34 → OUT 18 → DEMBED 1 → Dante 1–12
CG1 Mac (EC4) → IN 37 → OUT 20 → DEMBED 2 → Dante 25/26
  1. These audio paths de-embed from the same SDI feeds used for video — if picture is fine but audio is missing, the fault is isolated to the Sonifex AVN DIO 2 embedder/de-embedder stage or the Dante routing, not the SDI path.
  2. Check Sonifex AVN DIO 2 channel assignment and status for DEMBED 1 (Resi) or DEMBED 2 (CG1).
  3. Confirm the console's Dante patch still has channels 1–12 (Resi) / 25–26 (CG1) assigned to the correct input strip.
